About us


The Science Blog Pond was created by post-grad students from the University of Melbourne Science Communication class of 2014. Blogs are a great way of delivering scientific information to the public, but there are so many to choose from, and it can be difficult to find ones of interest. The team has utilised the iFish exploration engine in order to help people explore a number of different science blogs. iFish can help users find blogs that they are interested in, and many that they might not have discovered otherwise. The sliders provide a link between different personal preferences and the blog content in unique ways that other search engines such as Google cannot do. This allows people to easily find blogs that best suit their interests, without all of the tedious work.
